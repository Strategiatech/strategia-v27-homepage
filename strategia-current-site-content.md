### V-Parse(TM) -- Semantic CV Analysis Engine

**Description:** Transform unstructured data into structured intelligence. V-Parse(TM) goes beyond simple keyword matching to understand context, chronology, and competency depth.

**Features:**
- **Contextual Taxonomy** -- Maps over 40,000 skills across 140 languages, distinguishing between clinical certifications and specializations.
- **Format Agnostic** -- Ingests PDF, DOCX, HTML, and Image-based resumes with 99.8% OCR accuracy.
- **Bias Stripping** -- Automatically redacts PII (Personally Identifiable Information) to ensure merit-based screening.

**Stat:** 1,000+ CVs Processed / Hour

**Core Capabilities:** Ready to deploy. Integrate this module standalone or as part of the full suite.

---

### V-Psych(TM) -- Industrial-Organizational Profiling

**Description:** A proprietary assessment suite built by clinical psychologists. We measure the Big Five personality traits alongside cognitive aptitude to predict role fit.

**Features:**
- **OCEAN Methodology** -- Measures Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, and Neuroticism.
- **Adaptive Testing** -- Item Response Theory (IRT) adapts question difficulty in real-time for shorter, more accurate tests.
- **Role Benchmarking** -- Compare candidate profiles against high-performer baselines specific to your organization.

**Stat:** 0.85 Predictive Validity (r)

---

### V-Interview(TM) -- AI-Moderated Asynchronous Screening

**Description:** Standardize your first-round interviews. AI avatars guide candidates through structured questions, analyzing responses for sentiment, clarity, and content.

**Features:**
- **24/7 Availability** -- Candidates can complete interviews on their own schedule, reducing time-to-hire friction.
- **Sentiment Analysis** -- Detects confidence levels, stress markers, and positive/negative sentiment in real-time.
- **Anti-Cheating** -- Monitors for tab switching, multiple voices, and off-screen prompting to ensure integrity.

**Stat:** 60% Reduction in Screening Time
